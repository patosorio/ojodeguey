Stones


Ojo de grey uses genuine stones, that are personally selected and cut from the rough, ensuring that each piece has its own distinct personality, transforming our jewels in strong atemporal striking pieces, although always with a delicate allure.


Gems and Stones are gift from nature, and like everything that comes from the Pachamama they possess their own energy. With our work we want to convey the strength of the materials and the positive attitute we had in creating it. We seek to spread emotion.
